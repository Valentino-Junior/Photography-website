H-ART THE BAND ft Cedo

H-Art the Band is a Nairobi-based musical group. Mordecai Kimeu, Kenneth Muya, and Wachira Gatama make up the band. In 2012, the trio met during a Kenya National Theatre audition for a cancer-awareness show. In 2013, they created H-Art the Band after performing together for the first time at Dass Ethiopian restaurant.

Cedric Kadenyi, well known by his stage title Cedo as a Director and Producer for Pacho Group Limited in Nairobi.
